About Fusion

Fusion 2K25 is a 24-hour national level hackathon that brings together the brightest minds in technology to innovate, build, and compete. Organized by IIC E-Cell SKNCOE and SKNISB IEEE Student Branch, this event focuses on fostering innovation, coding excellence, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.

Participants work tirelessly through the night, mentored by industry experts, to create groundbreaking solutions that can make a real impact.
